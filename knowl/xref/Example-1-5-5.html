Simplify \(6 + 2 \cdot 5 - 12 \div 3 \cdot 2\)
Solution.
We start by underlining each term of the expression separately. Then we simplify each term.
\begin{align*} \underline{6} \amp + \underline{2 \cdot 5} - \underline{12 \div 3 \cdot 2} \amp \amp \blert{\text{Perform multiplications and divisions from left to right.}}\\ \amp = 6 + 10 - 8 \amp \amp \blert{\text{Perform additions and subtractions from left to right.}}\\ \amp = 16 - 8 = 8 \end{align*}
