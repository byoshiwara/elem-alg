Activity 3.6. Calculating Slope.

To calculate slope, we choose two points on the graph and compute the ratio
\begin{equation*} \dfrac{\blert{\text{change in vertical coordinate}}} {\blert{\text{change in horizontal coordinate}}} \end{equation*}
Be sure to include units with your ratios!
