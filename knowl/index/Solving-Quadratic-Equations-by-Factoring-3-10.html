Factors of a Quadratic Trinomial.

  1. The quadratic term, \(x^2\text{,}\) comes from the product of the First terms in each binomial.
  2. The constant term, \(12\text{,}\) is the product of the Last terms.
  3. The middle term, \(7x\text{,}\) is the sum of two terms, the product of the Inside terms and the product of the Outside terms.
