Laws of Exponents.

  1. \(\displaystyle \blert{~a^m \cdot a^n = a^{m+n}}\)
  2. \(\blert{~\dfrac{a^m}{a^n} = a^{m-n}~~~~(n \lt m)}\)
    \(\blert{~\dfrac{a^m}{a^n} = \dfrac{1}{a^{n-m}}~~~~(n \gt m)}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \blert{~(a^m)^n = a^{mn}}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle \blert{~(ab)^n = a^nb^n}\)
  5. \(\displaystyle \blert{~\left(\dfrac{a}{b}\right)^n = \dfrac{a^n}{b^n}}\)
